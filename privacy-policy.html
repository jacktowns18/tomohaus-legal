Information We Collect

TRKD Plus collects the following categories of information. All of it is provided by you directly, and all of it is used only to operate the app.

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account recovery
  • Password — stored only as a secure hash via Firebase Authentication; never readable by us or anyone else
  • Display name (optional) — shown in the app interface

2.2 Profile Information

You may optionally add the following profile details:

  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Height and weight (with unit preference)
  • Experience level
  • Goals (e.g., fitness or wellness objectives)
  • Allergies
  • Blood type
  • Healthcare provider name
  • Profile photo (avatar)

All profile fields are optional. Providing them enables features like personalized body diagrams, age-aware displays, and progress tracking. You can edit or remove this information at any time from the in-app Profile page.

2.3 Health and Wellness Data

TRKD Plus is a personal tracking tool. The core health data you enter includes:

  • Compound and protocol information — the substances, doses, frequencies, and schedules you personally track
  • Dose logs — date, time, amount, injection site, notes, and any side effects you record
  • Body measurements — body weight over time, body fat percentage, muscle mass
  • Journal entries — mood ratings (1–5 scale) and any notes you write
  • Progress photos — images you choose to upload
  • Goal weight and related progress targets

This data is private to your account and is not accessible to other users.

2.4 App Usage Data Stored On Your Device

To improve your experience, the app stores small amounts of data locally on your device:

  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Notification toggle state
  • Celebration/milestone state (to avoid duplicate notifications)
  • Temporary email for magic-link sign-in (deleted immediately after use)

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us, except where noted as mirrored to your account (such as your notifications preference).

2.5 Data Collected Automatically by Our Service Provider

We use Firebase (a Google Cloud service) to operate the app's backend. As part of providing that service, Firebase's infrastructure may automatically log:

  • Your IP address (when connecting to authentication or database services)
  • Approximate geographic region derived from that IP address
  • Firebase SDK version and platform information

We do not use this data for analytics, marketing, or tracking. It is collected by Firebase's infrastructure for security, abuse prevention, and service delivery, and we do not ingest or analyze it ourselves. Firebase's handling of this data is governed by Google's privacy policy.

We do not use Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance Monitoring, or any other analytics product.

What We Do Not Collect

For clarity, TRKD Plus does not collect:

  • Precise or GPS location data
  • Contact lists or address book information
  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Information from other apps on your device
  • Browsing history
  • Biometric identifiers (such as fingerprints or face geometry)
  • Financial account or payment information (the app is free with no in-app purchases as of v1.0)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Operate the app's core features — authenticate your account, display your logs and history, render body diagrams, calculate adherence and streaks, send in-app notifications you have enabled
  • Sync your data across your devices — so your logs are available wherever you sign in
  • Maintain your account — including account recovery, password reset, and deletion requests
  • Protect the security of the service — detect and prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations — such as responding to valid legal requests

We do not use your data to serve advertisements, build profiles for marketing, sell or rent to third parties, train machine-learning models, share with data brokers, or track you across other apps or websites.

How We Share Your Information

We use Firebase (Google Cloud) as our backend platform. Firebase acts as a data processor on our behalf, providing authentication, database, and file storage services. Your data is stored in Firebase under your account and is not accessible to other Firebase customers or to Google for independent purposes. Google's processing is subject to its own privacy and data processing terms, which you can review at Google Cloud's Data Processing Addendum.

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, subpoena, or valid legal process, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

5.3 Business Transfers

If Tomo Haus LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you by email and/or a prominent notice in the app before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share your personal information with third parties for advertising or cross-context behavioral advertising purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account through the in-app Delete Account option (available in your Profile), we delete your account record, profile data, compound and dose logs, journal entries, biometric logs, weight logs, and progress photos from Firestore and Firebase Storage.

Certain technical logs retained by Firebase (such as authentication logs) may persist for a limited period in accordance with Firebase's standard retention practices, but are not used by us for any purpose beyond security and service integrity.

Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • All data transmitted between the app and our backend is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Data stored in Firebase is encrypted at rest by Google Cloud.
  • Passwords are hashed by Firebase Authentication and never stored or visible in plaintext.
  • Access to your account requires authentication with your email and password or a valid magic link.
  • Firebase Security Rules enforce that your data can only be read or written by you (when authenticated).

No system is perfectly secure. If we become aware of a security incident affecting your personal information, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.
